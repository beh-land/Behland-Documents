## ğŸ¦ Financial Transparency

BehLand maintains a structured wallet architecture:
- A **main treasury wallet** used for long-term reserves and presale funds
- An **operational wallet** used for day-to-day expenses

The treasury wallet is protected using **multi-signature authorization**, ensuring that no single individual can unilaterally move core funds.
All transactions are traceable on-chain via public explorers.
