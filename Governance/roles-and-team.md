This document outlines the roles and responsibilities within the BehLand ecosystem to ensure transparency and accountability. It serves as a reference for team structure and decision-making processes.

## Founder & Ecosystem Lead
**Responsibilities:**
- Vision and overall strategy
- Economic model and partnerships
- Resource acquisition and management
- Final decision-making authority

## Project Manager
**Responsibilities:**
- Project planning and timeline management
- Coordination between teams
- Quality control and output delivery
- Reporting to stakeholders and investors

## Product / Growth
**Responsibilities:**
- Defining product features and roadmap
- Prioritizing the product backlog
- Marketing, communication, and content creation
- Managing user growth and engagement channels

## Technical Contributors
**Responsibilities:**
- Development and implementation of technical solutions
- Maintenance of infrastructure and codebase
- No executive decision-making power, purely technical contributions

## Advisors (if applicable)
**Responsibilities:**
- Providing strategic guidance and expertise
- No executive decision-making authority

## Decision Process
- Decisions related to the ecosystem vision, strategic direction, and major partnerships are made by the **Founder & Ecosystem Lead**.
- Operational decisions, product development, and growth-related actions are made by the **Project Manager** with team input.
- Decisions related to technical execution and codebase changes are decided by the **Technical Contributors**.
- Advisory input is considered but does not hold executive power.
