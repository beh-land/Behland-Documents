BehLand operates multiple wallets, each with a clearly defined role:

### Main Treasury Wallet
- Holds primary funds related to presales and long-term reserves
- Protected using **multi-signature authorization**
- Transactions require approval from multiple trusted signers
- No single individual has unilateral control over treasury funds

### Operational Wallet
- Used for day-to-day operational expenses
- Funded periodically from the treasury
- Subject to internal spending controls

## ğŸ”‘ Access Control & Permissions

BehLand follows a principle of **separated responsibilities**:

- **Owner-level actions** are restricted and limited
- **Administrative actions** are scoped and role-based
- Critical operations require multi-party approval
- There are no unrestricted single-signer permissions over core assets

This structure reduces single points of failure and operational risk.
